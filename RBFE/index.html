Relative Dissociation Free Energy calculations on HPCs: tutorial for GROMACS users


This is a technical tutorial for the calculation of the relative dissociation free energies for the SAMPL9/WP6 host-guest systems on high performing architectures using a nonequilibrium alchemical approach with the GROMACS code. The theoretical background of the methodology can be found here .
